Our Journey

Kashmiri Dhaage was founded to celebrate the timeless beauty of embroidery and fabrics, bringing luxury ethnic wear to women who cherish tradition with a modern touch.

Every outfit is handcrafted with intricate chikankari, gota, zari, zardozi, and mirror work, using premium fabrics like lawn, organza, and net.

We promise authenticity, quality, and designs that make you feel confident, elegant, and celebrated at every occasion.
